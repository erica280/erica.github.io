    Challenge #5: Accessibility Statement

    For Challenge #4, the primary accessibility concern would be surrounding the small subtext underneath the title of each page. This presents some concerns because those with poor vision, such as older people, may find difficulty with reading the small subtext. A solution to this could be to reduce the amount of subtext seen and make the font larger. For the dark mode, the gray and blue colors of the subtext also present similar concerns. This could be fixed by changing the color to white.
