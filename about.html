About Me

My journey first began in the world of film because I love expressing new perspectives on ordinary things. I then transitioned into the Environmental Design program at the University of Colorado Boulder because I took interest in designing for a more sustainable future. My studies in architecture have lead me through a series of explorations that have driven me to become invested in designing meaningful and thoughtful spaces that enhance the everyday experience. Through film, design, or architecture, as long as I am creating and working towards some kind of positive social change, I am happy.
